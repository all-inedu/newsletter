TECHNOLOGY: THE DEFINING SECTOR OF THE 21ST CENTURY

Planning to get your hands in computer science? With a forecasted growth of 15% in the employment realm, you really can't go wrong with computer science. Find out which universities that offer top-notch programs in computer science.

Have you ever wondered how everything in your phone works? Well, it’s all thanks to the hands of developers! From creating software, writing codes, to designing apps, developers play a major role in the tech sector.

Debug everything you should know about technology, right in our blog!

It should come as no surprise that the tech industry is the future given how much it dominates our lives. With this in mind, you might be wondering how to dip your toes in the tech world.

Save your worries! Kristian, an ALL-in mentor, is sharing some tips on how to dive into the tech world. Let’s find out:

    1. Identify your niche within tech

    The tech industry is diverse, broad and rapidly expanding. So, be specific on which area of tech you want to pursue. Plan ahead both the type of company and the specific role you want within tech.

    2. Be clear, specific and unique in building your profile!

    In reality you will have many competitors in the tech world. To find your way to successfully stand out, you want to communicate what sets you apart from others.

You can identify your unique value proposition by reflecting on the areas where you constantly receive praise.

Additionally, here are basic technical tips you should know and master for the tech world:

    1. Master the keyboard: The faster you can type accurately, the better.

    2. Learn software basics: This program should be able to help you in your work (Microsoft office, photo and video editing, basic coding, etc)
