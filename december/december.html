Finals are over and school’s out. Aside from enjoying your time with your family and friends, you may be wondering what to do with all that free time. We got your back!

Don’t let your holiday slip away easily. Find out how to stay productive during the holidays from Andra, an ALL-in mentor:

  • Reflect and refocus

    • It’s a good time to reflect on how you did in the previous semester, challenges you faced, and how you overcame them. Reflect on your growth and how you will approach the next semester better.

    • You can set up new goals for next semester to ensure you are focusing on building your required skill sets and knowledge for your university application

  • Try that new activity you have always been wanting to do

    • Since it's the holiday season, do find something fun for you to do to refresh yourself. Pick up that new hobby you have always been wanting to do, dwell in it and see how you really like it. It’s always good to use your time to find out what sparks joy in your life.

    • This new activity can also be like taking a trip and still incorporating your interest in it to discover or learn something new.

    • Volunteering or doing a personal project during the holiday season is also a great thing to do. Just make sure you balance it out with enough rest.

Success Story

Most stray animals depend on humans for their life, including dogs. Abian, who currently lives in Oman, has seen many people that volunteer to feed stray dogs in the street there. That’s when he came up with an idea to make it more convenient for these people who have already done amazing initiatives.

He’s currently working on a project to create an automatic dog feeder that dispenses food every couple of hours. This project has been going for less than three months, and the prototype for this device is currently in the making. He has already finished making the build and progressing on researching the circuit aspect now.

Like Abian, you can do your own project too! Make the most of your holiday by creating something that could contribute to the community and fulfill your hunger for passion.
