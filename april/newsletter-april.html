Whether you're just starting to write one or you're in the middle of writing one, we've got you covered! Chiara, an ALL-in mentor, is sharing some tips regarding admission essays. If you're in need of some guidance, then this one's for you!

  • Step 1: Know where you’re applying to!

    It seems obvious, but knowing the university (and even the country) you’re applying to makes all the difference. Different countries have different approaches to admission essays, and knowing that may be the reason you get accepted!

  • Step 2: Answer the prompt

    It can be really easy to get sidetracked when you’re 300 words into your essay. Keep the prompt at the top of your word document so you can constantly refer to it and make sure that your essay is still responding to the prompt.

  • Step 3: Research your university! Like, really research it.

    Some universities will ask you to write an essay about why you chose to apply to their university. Do thorough research into the course you’re applying for: are there any classes that pique your interest, or any professors whose work you like? Write about this to show that you’ve done your research, and your interest in the university is genuine. This is your chance to shine and really put yourself above other applicants.
